How many nuts will be left after division Open on JetBrains Academy

N squirrels found K nuts and decided to divide them equally. Find how many nuts will be left after each of the squirrels takes the equal amount of nuts.

Input data format

There are two positive integers N and K, neither of them are greater than 10000.


Sample Input:
3
14
Sample Output:
2


Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 1 second
